Debrief

This dialogue gives further information about the mission and target highlighted on the Mission Results Dialogue. The form of the dialogue will depend on the type of mission.

To get further information about kills and losses, you should go to the Squadron dialogue. Clicking on the Losses button will list the pilots in your command that have been killed. Clicking on any of the pilots on the Squadron dialogue will open the pilot details for that pilot’s flight. The kills on the last mission are displayed as well as the total kills.
