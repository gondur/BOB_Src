Flight Details

This Dialogue is launched when you click on a pilot on the Squadron Dialogue. This Dialogue displays the details of the four pilots in the clicked pilot’s flight.

There are two tabs on this Dialogue. The Personal tab information summarizes the important facts about the pilots. This information is vital when deciding on promotions, demotions and allocating rest days.

Click on the aircraft tabs to display the artwork painted on the side of the pilot’s aircraft. Click on a piece of artwork to see the full range of options.
