These are the notes associated with the currently highlighted item in the Daily Intelligence Summary dialogue.Target List

This Dialogue displays intelligence information about the recognized targets in North Korea. The targets have been allocated to the following categories:

Supply

These are the key locations on the Communists supply routes. Typically the targets are either marshalling yards or warehouses.

Choke

These are the bridges on the main supply routes

Traffic

This list shows the key supply routes for the Communist forces.

Airfields

This list shows the airfields available to the Communist forces.

Army

This form is different from the other four. Instead of showing a list of targets, information about the frontline is shown.

Targets

In the case of Supply, Choke, Traffic and Airfields, the information is arranged into lists that can be sorted according to the following criteria:
MSR: East

MSR: Central

MSR: West

Activity

Capacity

MSR, which stands for Main Supply Route, is explained in Battle Scenarios.

There are two buttons at the bottom of the list:

Dossier

Click on this button to display the Dossier for the highlighted target.

Authorize

Click on this button to generate a mission against the highlighted target.

Front Line

The front line is divided into three areas: west, central and east. Use the tick buttons to select the area that you want to view.

For each area, a summary of the position at the front line is presented, together with a set of three buttons:

Dossier

Click on this button to display the Dossier for the nearest target on the supply route to this front line.

Details

Click on this button to get more detailed information about the front line.

Authorize

Click on this button to authorize a close air support mission for this area of the front line.
