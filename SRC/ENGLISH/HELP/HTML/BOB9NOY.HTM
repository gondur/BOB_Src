Mission Folder

The Mission Folder lists the missions planned for the current session. The various methods of initiating a mission are described in the Mission Generation note.

Click on a mission line to highlight it. There are four buttons at the bottom of the Mission Folder Dialogue:

Intelligence

Click on this button to display the Dossier for the highlighted mission’s target.

Profile

Click on this button to display more details about the highlighted mission.

Delete

Click on this button to delete the highlighted mission.

Frag

Click on this button to move to the Frag Screen.
