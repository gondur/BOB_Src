Overview

This Dialogue shows the scoreboard for the aircraft under your command. The player’s individual achievements are displayed on the Player Log.

The data are arranged on two tabs:
Ac Stats Aircraft Statistics

Ground Stats Ground Statistics


The radio buttons at the top of the Dialogue filter the statistics. The options available actually vary depending on the time of day. Essentially, the following options are offered:
Last session

Last full day

Since the campaign began

In each case, two separate lists are displayed: one for kills and one for losses.
